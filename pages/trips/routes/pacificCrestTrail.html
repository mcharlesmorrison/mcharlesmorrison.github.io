Pacific Crest Trail

May-August 2019

The summer of 2019 held many a twist and a turn. I had originally planned to bikepack across Arizona and Oregon, but a wedding I forgot about threw my schedule off. So, with a little over a week to plan, I decided to hit the Pacific Crest Trail, a 2,650 mile track from Mexico to Canada. The 84 days I spent on trail were some of the best of my life.
