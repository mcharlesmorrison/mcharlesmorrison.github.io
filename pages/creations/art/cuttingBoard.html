Cutting Board

A good gift for friends and family

Cutting boards make nice gifts for people. They are fun to make, and offer a great deal of design choices. I settled on this design a while back and have made several. I like how the rim around the edge looks, which I used a router to cut. It is made of Redwood (of course), with small rubber pads on the feet.
