Canadian Tire

The gnarliest trail in Santa Cruz

Canadian Tire is a new line off the top of of Inferno. It involves a north shore style skinny onto a downed Tan Oak tree, an interesting hip, a wood drop that jumps over Inferno, and the steepest and gnarliest chute in Santa Cruz. If that wasn't enough, it ends with a big and super techy drop that drops you back into the bottom section of Inferno. Trail preview below:
